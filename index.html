Poolingsystem
Already since Vanilla Wow Blizzard has integrated a pooling system. This uknown system, is even used in Molten Core. During AQ, Naxxramas and especially since TBC pools are used in dungeons and herbs.

But What ist this Pooling?
Pooling is best explained with an example. If two objects/NPCs are located in the same pool, there is a chance that either object/NPC X or Y is spawned. But never both at the same time! Here is an example of a Pool in Auchenai Crypts. Shown is the same Group but at two different IDs.

We from Team Burning-Tides took great effort to integrate a funtioncal pooling system in every TBC dungeon, so the game experience doesn't differ from the originial Blizzard TBC. Furthermore we guarantee with a running Pooling System a variety during your daily dungeons.
